## zh-CN

不展示秒，也不允许选择。

## en-US

The `seconds` options are hidden and cannot be selected.

````jsx
import { TimePicker } from 'antd';

ReactDOM.render(
  <TimePicker defaultValue="12:08:23" format="HH:mm" />
, mountNode);
````
